## Commission Strategy (B2B SaaS Focus)

The new focus revolves around **High-Ticket B2B Lead Gen**, rather than high-volume, low-payout consumer affiliate links. A single qualified demo or closed deal with a vendor like Drata or Wiz is worth 50x–100x more than a retail VPN sign-up.

## Editorial Rule: "Human-in-the-Loop" Trust

> **Editorial directive:** Affiliate links must feel like organic B2B tooling recommendations, seamlessly fitting into mitigation strategies. Elite security professionals trust content that provides architectural insights.
> **DO NOT inject consumer products (e.g. retail VPNs) into deeply technical LLM or MLSecOps threat briefings.**

- **Compliance articles**: Recommend Vanta and Drata for automating controls against new threat variants.
- **AI/LLM-specific threats**: Point directly to Lakera and Protect AI as specialized countermeasures.
- **Identity/IAM**: Recommend 1Password for Business and Auth0/Okta.
- **Cloud/Infra**: Pair Wiz and CrowdStrike with cloud data exfiltration vulnerabilities.
