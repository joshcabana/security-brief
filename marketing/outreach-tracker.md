## 1. CRM Template Structure (Google Sheets / Airtable)
Set up a standard pipeline board.

- **Columns**: 
  - `Company Name`
  - `Target Persona (DevRel, VP Marketing, Growth)`
  - `Contact Name & LinkedIn URL`
  - `Email (from Apollo/Hunter)`
  - `Status` (To Contact, Attempt 1, Attempt 2, Meeting Booked, Negotiating, Closed/Won)
  - `Angle Used` (Included in Matrix / Cold Pitch / Content Collab)
