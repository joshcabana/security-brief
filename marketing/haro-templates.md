# HARO Pitch Templates — AI Security Brief

Five pre-written pitch templates for Help A Reporter Out (HARO) responses.
Aim to respond to 2-3 relevant queries per day. Filter categories: Technology, Business.
Last updated: 26 March 2026

Sign-up URL: https://helpareporter.com

---

## How to Use These Templates

1. Log into HARO and filter for Technology and Business categories
2. Scan queries twice daily (morning + afternoon AEST)
3. Match query topics to the template below
4. Copy the template, customise the bracketed sections, and send within 24 hours of the query posting
5. Keep responses under 250 words — journalists scan fast
6. Always include your full byline and site URL

## Tips for Higher Placement Rate

- Respond within 2-4 hours of query posting (HARO sends 3x daily)
- Lead with your specific credentials, not generic statements
- Give 2-3 concrete, quotable insights — not a wall of text
- Always link to a relevant article for credibility
- Follow up once if no response within 5 days
- Track responses in a spreadsheet: date, outlet, query topic, outcome
