# AI Security Brief - Sponsorship Outreach Playbook

This document contains 3 ready-to-send cold outreach templates targeting enterprise security vendors ($1k–$3k per sponsored briefing).

## Core Pitch Mechanics
- **Audience**: 4,500+ active CISOs, Security Engineers, and Red Teamers.
- **Why this works**: Our audience is extremely technical. They do not click on generic SaaS ads. They buy products that solve specific agentic/MLSecOps vulnerabilities.
- **The Format**: Deep-dive technical sponsorship. Not a banner ad. We implement a proof-of-concept of their tool breaking down an exploit.

---

I run AI Security Brief, a specialized intelligence feed read by 4,500+ active CISOs, Red Teamers, and AppSec engineers.

Are you open to a brief chat about a sponsored placement where we showcase a technical deep-dive of your platform to our practitioners? Our technical placements currently run $1,500-$2,500 per issue.

Best,
Josh Cabana
Founder, AI Security Brief
