# The 30-Day Content Repurposing Calendar

**Goal**: Transform 1 core weekly briefing into 7 days of algorithmic distribution across X and LinkedIn. Every asset pushes traffic back to `/lead-magnet` or the `[beehiiv:paywall]`.

## 1. The Omni-Channel System (1 Briefing = 7 Assets)

Whenever a new briefing is published on Tuesday at 9:00 AM:

*   **Asset 1**: The Core Briefing (Web/Email).
*   **Asset 2 (X)**: A 5-7 part technical thread extracting the central "Threat Mechanism".
*   **Asset 3 (LinkedIn)**: A 6-slide PDF Carousel ("The 5 Ways to Mitigate [Threat]").
*   **Asset 4 (YouTube Short / Reels)**: A 45-second "Threat Breakdown" direct-to-camera script.
*   **Asset 5 (X)**: A controversial or contrarian "Hot Take" related to the post context.
*   **Asset 6 (LinkedIn)**: A text-only "Story/Lesson" post about how a vendor mismanaged the threat.
*   **Asset 7 (Newsletter)**: "Weekend Recap" blast routing back to the top tools from the matrix.

## 2. Standard Operation Templates

### X Thread Template
> **Tweet 1 (Hook)**: AI Agents aren't just reading data anymore. They are executing code. Here is exactly how the new [Threat Name] bypasses traditional WAFs, and what you need to do about it today. 🧵 [Image of code snippet or diagram]
> **Tweet 2 (Context)**: The traditional mechanism relied on X...
> **Tweet 3 (The Exploit)**: But with autonomous agents, the exploit works by Y...
> **Tweet 4 (The Fix)**: Here are the 3 architecture patterns to stop this: 1. A, 2. B, 3. C.
> **Tweet 5 (CTA)**: Want the full technical breakdown and exactly which tools solve this? Read the full 2026 threat briefing here: [Link]

### LinkedIn Carousel Template (Figma / Canva)
- **Slide 1**: Title (Big, bold, contrasting text): "Why your current WAF won't stop Agentic AI"
- **Slide 2**: The Problem (Stats, urgency).
- **Slide 3-5**: The Solution Steps (Actionable code or config screenshots).
- **Slide 6 (Final CTA)**: "Get the complete 2026 Threat Landscape PDF. Link in the comments."
