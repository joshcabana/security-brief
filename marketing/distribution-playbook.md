# Phase 4: Distribution & Traffic Playbook

The site is complete. We now shift from "building" to "distributing". The goal is to aggressively push top-of-funnel (TOFU) traffic to `/lead-magnet` and `/tools` to kickstart the Month 1-3 revenue ramp.

## 1. Repurposing Engine (Every Briefing)
Every time a new AI security briefing is published, it MUST be atomized into the following distribution assets:

*   **X (Twitter) Thread**: 
    *   *Hook*: Start with the impact / exploit. (e.g. "We just saw Prompt Injection bypass the leading WAF. Here is the exact payload and how to patch it.")
    *   *Body*: 4-5 tweets breaking down the technical mechanism.
    *   *CTA*: "Get the full 2026 Threat Landscape PDF and weekly patching tutorials: [Link to /lead-magnet]"
*   **LinkedIn Carousel**:
    *   Convert the briefing into a 5-6 slide PDF (dark cyber aesthetic). 
    *   LinkedIn algorithm heavily promotes document engagement.
    *   *CTA*: Link to the full article / `/lead-magnet` in the comments (never the main body to avoid reach penalties).
*   **YouTube Short / TikTok**:
    *   A 60-second screen-recording style breakdown of the threat. Use a tool like Remotion or simple OBS.

## 2. Weekly Posting Schedule (Cadence)
To maintain momentum and trigger algorithmic reach:
*   **Monday**: *The Insight* - High-level industry observation or upcoming threat prediction.
*   **Wednesday**: *The Teardown* - Post the core X Thread / LinkedIn Carousel derived from the week's newsletter.
*   **Friday**: *The Tooling Focus* - Highlight ONE tool from the `ToolsMatrix` that solves a specific problem. Share the affiliate link directly.

## 3. Newsletter Cross-Pollination (Swapstack)
We need to tap into adjacent audiences. Target newsletters via Swapstack or direct DM:
*   **Targets**: DevOps feeds, AI/ML engineering newsletters, Cloud Security overviews.
*   **The Pitch**: "I run AI Security Brief for 4.5k CISOs. I'd love to swap a recommendation slot in our upcoming issue for a shoutout in yours."
*   **CTA for Swaps**: Always direct them to `/lead-magnet` for the highest conversion rate.

## 4. Paid Acceleration ($500 LinkedIn Test)
Since the audience is highly specialized, LinkedIn Ads are viable *if* targeted with surgical precision.
*   **Budget**: $500 total ($50/day over 10 days).
*   **Audience**: Job titles: "CISO", "Security Engineer", "Head of Application Security", "Threat Intelligence Analyst".
*   **Creative**: High-contrast, dark mode image of the "2026 Agentic AI Threat Landscape" report.
*   **Offer**: "Free 2026 Report on AI vulnerabilities. Download now."
*   **Destination**: `/lead-magnet`
*   **Measurement**: Plausible Analytics will track campaign effectiveness. If CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) < $15 for a high-quality B2B subscriber, scale the budget immediately.
